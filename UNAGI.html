Unagi by YVKB

< BACK

Unagi is a high-end 60% mechanical keyboard kit, designed by myself for Yorkville Keyboards (YVKB). With an anodized aluminum top case and full stainless steel bottom case, Unagi serves as an understated centerpiece for any desk, meticulously designed to withstand the test of time.

Unagi was available in June of 2023. The base kit price was $450. Below, you will find prototype images, the project timeline, and my closing thoughts on the board.

Unagi Prototype 01

- Gray aluminum top
- Raw stainless steel bottom
- Manufacturer: Guangzhou Gaojie
- Received: 9.16.22

For the first machined prototype I had ever ordered, I was delighted by the fact that the board was functional; however, various aesthetic inconsistencies unfortunately prompted a second prototyping round.

Unagi Prototype 02

- Pink aluminum top
