XTAL is an ergonomic mechanical keyboard designed for Yorkville Keyboards, in collaboration with the wonderful Hadi. After Unagi, I sought to challenge myself further; parting from my other boards designed around universal circuit boards, XTAL utilizes a Hadi-designed PCB that support a new ergonomic layout, one that combines the functionality of a 60% with the comfort of the Alice layout, all with virtually no adjustment period.

The design of XTAL is inspired by the Frutiger Aero aesthetic, an idealistic view of the future spawned from the early 2000s. It is characterized by serene landscapes and sterile urban environments filled with motifs from the sea and the early internet.

As of July 2023, XTAL is in the final design and prototyping stages. Below, you will preliminary design images and the current project timeline.
